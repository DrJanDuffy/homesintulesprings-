Tule Springs Villages

Four Distinct Neighborhoods in One Master-Planned Community

Village 1

Northwest Village

Homes from $425,000

Northwest Village 1 offers a perfect blend of desert beauty and modern convenience. Adjacent to the Eglington Preserve, residents enjoy stunning natural views and direct trail access.

  • Single-family homes 2,000-3,500 sq ft
  • Adjacent to Eglington Preserve
  • Multiple parks and walking trails
  • Quick access to Bruce Woodbury Beltway (215)
  • New construction available

Village 2

Northeast Village

Homes from $450,000

Northeast Village 2 is the heart of family living in Tule Springs. Known for its excellent schools and community amenities, this village is perfect for growing families.

  • Variety of home sizes 1,800-3,200 sq ft
  • Close to planned K-8 school
  • Community park and recreation center
  • Easy access to shopping and dining
  • Family-friendly atmosphere

Village 3

South Village

Homes from $385,000

South Village 3 offers diverse housing options including active adult communities. Centrally located with excellent access to future commercial developments.

  • Mix of single-family and 55+ communities
  • Planned commercial and retail nearby
  • Connected trail network
  • Central location in Tule Springs
  • Most affordable village

Southeast Village

Homes from $395,000

Southeast Village 4 features the newest construction in Tule Springs with contemporary designs and modern amenities. Perfect for those seeking new homes with the latest features.

  • Newest construction in Tule Springs
