Welcome to Tule Springs

Nestled in the northern part of Las Vegas, Tule Springs is one of the most desirable master-planned communities in the region. With its thoughtfully designed villages, beautiful desert landscaping, and abundance of amenities, it offers residents an exceptional quality of life.

As your dedicated Tule Springs expert, Dr. Jan Duffy has intimate knowledge of each village's unique characteristics, helping you find the perfect neighborhood to match your lifestyle.

Villages of Tule Springs

Explore the distinct villages that make up this premier community

Northwest Village 1

Northwest Village 1 offers a mix of single-family homes with convenient access to the Eglington Preserve. This area features homes with generous lot sizes and beautiful views of the surrounding desert landscape.

  • Single-family homes from 2,000-3,500 sq ft
  • Adjacent to natural preserve areas
  • Parks and walking trails
  • Quick access to Bruce Woodbury Beltway
View Northwest Village Listings

Northeast Village 2

Northeast Village 2 is known for its family-friendly atmosphere and proximity to community amenities. This village features a variety of home styles to accommodate different lifestyles and preferences.

  • Variety of home sizes from 1,800-3,200 sq ft
  • Close to planned elementary school
  • Community park and recreation areas
  • Easy access to shopping and dining
View Northeast Village Listings

South Village 3

South Village 3 offers a mix of housing options including some active adult communities. This area is designed with convenience in mind, with access to planned commercial areas and community facilities.

  • Mix of single-family and active adult communities
  • Planned commercial and retail access
  • Community trails connecting to regional network
  • Central location within Tule Springs
View South Village Listings

Southeast Village 4

Southeast Village 4 is the most recently developed area offering contemporary home designs with modern amenities. This village is perfect for those looking for new construction with the latest features.

  • Newest construction in Tule Springs
  • Modern floor plans from 2,000-3,800 sq ft
  • Energy-efficient home designs
  • Proximity to future school sites
View Southeast Village Listings

Community Amenities

Enjoy the exceptional parks, trails, and amenities that make Tule Springs special

Parks & Preserves

Tule Springs features numerous parks and natural preserves, including the iconic Tule Springs Regional Park offering walking trails, picnic areas, and desert landscapes.

Education

The community has planned sites for elementary, middle, and high schools. Learn more about schools serving Tule Springs and boundaries.

Shopping & Dining

With easy access to shopping centers and restaurants, Tule Springs residents enjoy convenience without sacrificing their peaceful neighborhood atmosphere.

Trails & Recreation

An extensive network of walking and biking trails connects the villages, encouraging an active lifestyle while enjoying the beautiful desert surroundings.

Community Design

Thoughtfully planned with desert-adapted landscaping, wide streets, and architectural guidelines that ensure beautiful, cohesive neighborhoods.

Location

Just minutes from the Las Vegas Strip with easy access to major highways, Tule Springs offers the perfect balance of suburban tranquility and urban convenience.
